## Why This Matters for Your Architecture

### The Photonic Problem

Your photonic D²NN computes instantly — light travels at c.
But it has **no memory**. Each forward pass is independent.
The interference patterns (MESO) emerge in space, not time.

### The Phononic Solution

Sound waves are **slow** (~1500 m/s in water vs 3×10⁸ m/s for light).
They **persist** in the medium — ringing like a bell after being struck.
They **interact** with the material — creating a "memory" of past states.

### The Optoacoustic Marriage

Light creates sound (photoacoustic effect).
Sound modulates light (acousto-optic effect).

This gives you **temporal depth**:
- Fast computation (photonic)
- Persistent memory (phononic)
- Contextual cognition (optoacoustic)
